About the Project

The UFV Run Club App began after a generous donation supporting a non-profit initiative aimed at transforming health, wellness, and community connection at the University of the Fraser Valley.

The app demonstrates how technology can be ethical, transparent, and fully focused on student well-being — unlike traditional fitness apps designed for profit.

Our Mission

Ethical Technology

The platform is built using ethical principles that protect privacy, promote fairness, and ensure transparency across all features.

Non-Profit Focus

Every dollar raised is reinvested into UFV scholarships, mental health support, and student-led events that strengthen the campus community.

Community Wellness

Encourages movement, connection, and healthy lifestyles through inclusive weekly run events and friendly challenges.

Student-Centered Design

Built by UFV students, for UFV students — focusing on accessibility, simplicity, and long-term community impact.

Who Is This For?

  • UFV students
  • UFV staff and faculty
  • UFV alumni
  • Community members supporting UFV wellness
