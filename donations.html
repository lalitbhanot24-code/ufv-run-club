Donations & Funding

The UFV Run Club App operates as a non-profit initiative. Every dollar raised supports student success, wellness, and community development at UFV.

Student Scholarships

Donations fund bursaries and scholarships, providing financial support to UFV students who need it most.

Mental Health Programs

Contributions help create and expand wellness programs focused on stress reduction, fitness, and mental well-being.

Community Outreach

Funds support events, partnerships, and local initiatives that strengthen the Fraser Valley community.
