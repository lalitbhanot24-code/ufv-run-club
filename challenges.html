Friendly Challenges

Challenges motivate our runners and help build a fun, competitive, and encouraging environment for everyone — whether you're a beginner or a seasoned athlete.

UFV 5K Starter

A beginner-friendly challenge: complete a total of 5 km in one week. Walk, jog, or run — every step counts!

Saturday Streak

Run every Saturday for one month and earn a digital badge and recognition on the community leaderboard.

Team Relay

Work together with friends to reach a combined distance of 100 km. Perfect for building teamwork and community spirit.

Alumni vs Students

A friendly competition where UFV alumni and students race to see who can log the most kilometres. Bragging rights included!
