Run Tracker

The Run Tracker will allow you to log your weekly runs, track progress, and compare your stats over time.

Planned Features

  • Simple form to log date, distance, and time.
  • A dashboard showing your total weekly, monthly, and yearly kilometres.
  • Progress charts to visualize improvement.
  • Option to export or share your progress with friends.
  • Integration with smartwatch/phone APIs (future version).
