Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to be a serious runner?

No. Walkers, joggers, new runners, and elite runners are all welcome! The UFV Run Club is built for everyone.

Is there a cost to use the app?

The core features are completely free. Optional donations and sponsorships directly support UFV students and community wellness programs.

What happens to my data?

We collect the minimum data required to track your runs. We do not sell or share your personal information. Everything is handled transparently and ethically.

Can I participate if I don’t have a smartwatch?

Absolutely! You can manually log your runs directly in the app.

How often do I need to run?

The program tracks weekly Saturday runs, but you can run anytime you want.

Is this really non-profit?

Yes. All revenue supports UFV students through scholarships, wellness programs, and community initiatives.
