Building a Stronger UFV Community

The UFV Run Club App is more than a fitness platform — it’s a space for students, employees, alumni, and local residents to come together, build connections, and support one another through shared health and wellness goals.

Weekly Social Runs

Join our Saturday community runs to stay motivated, active, and connected. All fitness levels are welcome — walk, jog, or run!

Meet New Friends & Mentors

Build meaningful friendships with peers, faculty, alumni, and community leaders who share a passion for wellness.

Wellness Support Network

Participate in a safe, encouraging environment that promotes mental, physical, and emotional well-being.

Local Business Partnerships

Enjoy events and sponsorships supported by Fraser Valley businesses, creating a stronger relationship between UFV and the community.
