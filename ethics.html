Ethical Foundations

The UFV Run Club App is designed using ethical frameworks from CIS and applied ethics courses. Our mission is to show how technology can be built responsibly, transparently, and for the public good.

Fairness & Accessibility

No paywalls. No targeting vulnerable populations. Every UFV student, worker, and community member can participate equally.

Privacy & Consent

We collect only the minimum required data and provide clear options for consent. No selling or sharing of personal information.

Non-Profit Commitment

All profits support UFV students through scholarships, wellness programs, and community initiatives — not corporations.

Transparency

Funding, data practices, project goals, and governance are explained in simple language. Users always know how the app operates.

In the full class presentation, each of the 9.5 ethical theories will be connected to these app features, demonstrating how the UFV Run Club App upholds ethical design principles.
